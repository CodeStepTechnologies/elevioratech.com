Platform Overview

TASTR is built to transform food delivery by prioritizing affordability, scalability, and modern infrastructure.

The platform integrates smart ordering, verified student discounts, group ordering, and flexible delivery modes, while supporting future expansion into grocery, healthcare, and essential services.
