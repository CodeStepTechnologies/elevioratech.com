Innovation Portfolio

Eleviora Tech Innovations designs, builds, and operates scalable global digital platforms across multiple industries. Each platform is engineered using modern architecture, cloud-native infrastructure, and performance-first engineering principles.

TASTR

TASTR is a next-generation delivery platform built for students and designed for global scalability. The platform enables smart ordering, verified student discounts, group orders, and flexible delivery infrastructure.

Launching across the United Kingdom and India, TASTR is designed to expand into multiple delivery verticals including food, grocery, healthcare essentials, and more.

CodeStep Technologies

CodeStep Technologies is an enterprise software and digital platform engineering company delivering scalable applications, modern infrastructure systems, and branding solutions.

The platform supports businesses globally through software development, mobile applications, enterprise systems, and digital transformation services.

Vayuara Holidays

Vayuara Holidays is a global travel technology platform providing booking infrastructure across flights, hotels, holidays, insurance, and visa services.

Designed with scalable infrastructure, the platform supports global travel operations and delivers seamless digital travel experiences.
